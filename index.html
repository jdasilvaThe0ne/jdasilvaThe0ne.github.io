Robotics & Autonomous Systems

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that combines mechanical design, electronics, control theory, and software engineering to create intelligent machines capable of sensing, decision-making, and action.

Modern robotics systems increasingly rely on middleware frameworks such as ROS 2 (open source) to manage communication, scalability, and real-time constraints across distributed components. They are becomings widely used in manufacturing industries, with it's opeen source nature,everyone to use as it, whitout limitation. Persons in the commuity can also Publish to the software. Over the years this has proved to be fruitful to both researchers and manufactuting industries globaly. Why ROS 2?

ROS 2 is a middleware designed to support real-world robotic systems. It emphasizes reliability, security, and real-time performance, which is crucial in enablling the robot to interact with its environment smoothly

Additionally,standardized interfaces, ROS 2 enables developers to transition seamlessly from simulation to deployment on physical robots.
